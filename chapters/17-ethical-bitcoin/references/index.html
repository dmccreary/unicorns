    References: Ethical Bitcoin — A Contradiction in Horns

    1. Bitcoin - Wikipedia - Comprehensive overview of Bitcoin's technology, economics, and controversies, providing the factual foundation for this chapter's examination of whether "ethical Bitcoin" is an oxymoron.

    2. Cryptocurrency and crime - Wikipedia - Documentation of cryptocurrency's role in fraud, ransomware, money laundering, and sanctions evasion, the ethical challenges this chapter argues cannot be engineered away.

    3. Environmental impact of cryptocurrency - Wikipedia - Research on crypto mining's carbon footprint, e-waste generation, and water consumption, the environmental costs the chapter's "free-range, organic" unicorn ranch cannot offset.

    4. Digital Gold: Bitcoin and the Inside Story of the Misfits and Millionaires Trying to Reinvent Money - Nathaniel Popper - Harper - Narrative history of Bitcoin's early community and ideological foundations, capturing the genuine utopianism this chapter finds tragically naive.

    5. Number Go Up: Inside Crypto's Wild Rise and Staggering Fall - Zach Faux - Crown Currency - Bloomberg journalist's investigation of the crypto industry's human cost, from Tether's opacity to FTX's collapse, the real-world wreckage behind the chapter's satire.

    6. Bitcoin's Energy Problem - Nature Climate Change - Peer-reviewed research estimating Bitcoin mining could push global warming above 2°C, the scientific basis for the chapter's environmental critique.

    7. Banking the Unbanked - World Bank - Data on global financial inclusion, measuring actual progress against crypto's recurring promise to provide banking to the world's poorest populations.

    8. Crypto Carbon Ratings Institute - CCRI - Independent assessment of cryptocurrency networks' environmental impact, the accountability mechanism the chapter's fictional unicorn ranch conspicuously lacks.

    9. FTX Collapse Explained - Investopedia - Timeline of crypto's most spectacular ethical failure, demonstrating that the chapter's satire about crypto ethics barely exaggerates reality.

    10. Proof of Stake vs Proof of Work - Ethereum Foundation - Technical comparison of consensus mechanisms and their environmental trade-offs, the engineering question beneath the chapter's ethical one.
