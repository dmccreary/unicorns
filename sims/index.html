    List of MicroSims

    Interactive Micro Simulations to help students explore mythical beasts, AI hype, technology fantasy, and the fine art of spotting unicorns in the wild.

    • Adaptive Learning Feedback Loop

      Step-through visualization showing how an adaptive learning system adjusts content based on student performance.

    • AGI Timeline Claims History

      Interactive timeline comparing AGI predictions against actual AI milestones, revealing the persistent gap.

    • AI Hype Cycle with Mythical Beasts

      Interactive hype cycle curve with AI technologies plotted and mythical beasts marking each phase.

    • AI Pitch Deck Anatomy

      Interactive pitch deck viewer where students evaluate claims on each slide using a traffic-light rating system.

    • AI Unicorn Feature Generator

      Configure an AI-enhanced unicorn by selecting features and see the resulting satirical venture capital valuation.

    • Ancient Unicorn Civilizations Map

      Interactive map showing historical unicorn sightings and cultural references from 2600 BCE to Silicon Valley.

    • Beast Allegory Network

      Network graph showing 11 mythical beasts connected to their real-world allegorical counterparts.

    • Beast Classification Framework

      Interactive radar chart for comparing mythical beast characteristics across six dimensions.

    • Beast Classifier

      Adjust six sliders to create a beast profile and find the closest matching mythical creature.

    • Bestiary of Vaporware Field Guide

      Dual-framework classification tool assigning both a mythical beast and vaporware level to technologies.

    • Blockchain Vaporware Classifier

      Drag blockchain applications onto a five-level vaporware spectrum from Shipping Product to Pure Unicorn.

    • Centaur Collaboration Spectrum

      Select a job role and adjust task characteristics to see where it falls on the augmentation-to-replacement spectrum.

    • Claim Verification Decision Tree

      Navigate a decision tree to systematically evaluate whether an AI claim is verified, plausible, or unsupported.

    • Education Technology Adoption Lag

      Horizontal bar chart comparing technology adoption lag in education from calculators to generative AI.

    • Five Years Away Timeline

      Dual-track timeline showing quantum computing predictions versus actual milestones.

    • Graph Viewer

      Interactive learning graph viewer showing concept dependencies and relationships.

    • Great Mythical Venn Diagram

      Drag 15 concept cards into the correct region of a three-circle Venn diagram: Fictional, Real, or Both.

    • Hype Cycle Journey

      Step-through interactive showing a deer's journey through the five phases of the technology hype cycle.

    • How a Large Language Model Works

      Seven-stage step-through visualization of how an LLM processes a prompt and generates text.

    • LinkedIn Skill Inflation Tracker

      Dual-line chart comparing growth of LinkedIn AI skill claims against actual job postings requiring those skills.

    • Media Literacy Evaluation Framework

      Evaluate AI media examples using a five-step checklist covering source, claims, incentives, and omissions.

    • Medieval Unicorn Belief Network

      Network graph showing connections between medieval beliefs about unicorns, from purity to royal symbolism.

    • Metaverse Hype Cycle History

      Four overlapping hype curves showing the repeating boom-bust pattern of VR and metaverse cycles.

    • Modern Unicorn Economy

      Horizontal bar chart showing the distribution of unicorn startups across industry sectors.

    • Moving the Goalposts Timeline

      Interactive timeline pairing AI achievements with the dismissals that followed each milestone.

    • Perpetual Beta Product Lifecycle

      Dual-line chart comparing traditional software maturity curves against perpetual beta products.

    • Product-Market Fit Spectrum

      Drag startup scenario cards to their correct position on a spectrum from Verified PMF to Mythical PMF.

    • Real or Fake Interactive Quiz

      Classify 12 statements as real quotes, fictional textbook quotes, or press release language.

    • Unicorn Lifecycle Allegory

      Step-through showing a startup's lifecycle mapped to different mythical beasts at each funding stage.

    • Unicorn Population Dynamics

      Population dynamics simulator modeling unicorn startup populations affected by belief, funding, and reality.

    • Unicorn Symbolism Timeline

      Interactive timeline tracing unicorn sightings and cultural significance from 2600 BCE to the present.

    • Unicorn Valuation Growth Chart

      Dual-axis chart showing the exponential growth in both the number and combined valuation of unicorn startups.
