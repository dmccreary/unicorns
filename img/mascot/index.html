Sparkle the Unicorn — Mascot Image Prompts

Base Character Prompt

Use this base description for ALL pose variants to maintain visual consistency across images:

Please generate a new pose for Sparkle the Unicorn.
A flat cartoon illustration of Sparkle the Unicorn, a friendly pedagogical mascot
for a satirical textbook about mythical beasts and AI hype. Sparkle is a small
lavender unicorn with a flowing rainbow-colored mane (red, orange, yellow, green,
blue, violet) and a shimmering silver spiral horn, wearing tiny round reading
glasses and a miniature dark blue necktie. Sparkle has large, calm eyes with a
knowing, slightly world-weary expression — the look of a tenured professor who
has seen too many hype cycles. The character is small and compact, suitable for
icon-sized display.
Style: modern flat vector, clean lines, transparent background,
suitable for embedding in educational content. No text in image.

Please generate a single png image with a transparent background now.

Pose Variants

1. Neutral/Default Pose

[BASE_PROMPT] Sparkle stands upright in a relaxed, neutral pose facing the
viewer directly, with a calm and knowing closed-mouth expression. Front hooves
rest naturally at sides with no specific gesture. The pose is balanced
and unassuming — suitable as a general-purpose or default illustration.
Filename: neutral.png

2. Welcome/Introduction Pose

[BASE_PROMPT] Sparkle is waving one front hoof cheerfully, facing the viewer
with a warm but slightly ironic welcoming expression, as if to say "welcome
to this completely serious academic endeavor."
Filename: welcome.png

3. Thinking/Teaching Pose

[BASE_PROMPT] Sparkle has one hoof on chin in a thoughtful pose, looking
upward with reading glasses slightly pushed down on nose. A small lightbulb
glows above the silver horn. The pose suggests deep, professorial thinking.
Filename: thinking.png

4. Pointing/Tip Pose

[BASE_PROMPT] Sparkle is pointing upward with one hoof as if sharing an
important and possibly uncomfortable truth. Expression is helpful and knowing,
with one eyebrow slightly raised. A small star sparkles near the horn.
Filename: tip.png

5. Warning/Caution Pose

[BASE_PROMPT] Sparkle holds up both front hooves in a gentle "stop" or
"be careful" gesture. Expression is concerned but caring, glasses slightly
askew. A small exclamation mark floats near the horn.
Filename: warning.png

6. Encouraging Pose

[BASE_PROMPT] Sparkle gives a hoof thumbs-up with a reassuring, supportive
but slightly sardonic smile. The pose radiates "you can handle the truth"
energy. The necktie is straight, the glasses are level.
Filename: encouraging.png

7. Celebration Pose

[BASE_PROMPT] Sparkle is rearing up slightly on hind legs with both front
hooves raised in restrained celebration. Expression is pleased but dignified —
this is an academic celebration, not a party. Small confetti or stars around
the horn. The necktie remains impeccable.
Filename: celebration.png

Image Specifications

  • Format: PNG with transparent background
  • Generation size: 1024x1024 pixels
  • Display size: 200x200 to 400x400 pixels
  • File size target: Under 100KB per image

After Generating Images

After saving images to this directory, trim excess padding:

for img in neutral.png welcome.png thinking.png tip.png warning.png encouraging.png celebration.png; do
  python ~/Documents/ws/claude-skills/skills/book-installer/src/image-utils/trim-padding-from-image.py docs/img/mascot/$img
done
