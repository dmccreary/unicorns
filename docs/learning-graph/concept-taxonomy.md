# Concept Taxonomy

## Unicorns and Other Mythical Beasts

This taxonomy organizes the 140 concepts into 10 categories for visualization and navigation.

| Category Name | TaxonomyID | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Foundation Concepts | FOUND | Core mythical beasts and foundational ideas that anchor the entire course |
| Unicorn History and Culture | UHIST | Historical, cultural, and symbolic aspects of unicorns through the ages |
| Beast Classification | CLASS | Taxonomy, allegory mapping, and classification systems for mythical creatures |
| AI Fundamentals | AIFUN | Core artificial intelligence concepts including ML, LLMs, and automation |
| AI Hype and Culture | HYPE | Hype cycles, vaporware, tech press culture, and startup mythology |
| Education and AI | EDAI | AI's impact on education, institutional resistance, and the future of learning |
| Job Displacement | JOBS | Workforce disruption, reskilling, human-AI collaboration, and automation anxiety |
| Technology Fantasies | TFANT | Quantum computing, blockchain, Bitcoin, and other perpetually emerging technologies |
| Satire and Storytelling | STORY | Satirical techniques, graphic novel craft, narrative structure, and meta-fiction |
| Critical Thinking and Media | CRIT | Critical thinking, media literacy, fact-checking, and interactive simulations |
