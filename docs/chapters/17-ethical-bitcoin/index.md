# Ethical Bitcoin: A Contradiction in Horns

## Summary

Can Bitcoin be ethical? Can a unicorn be ordinary? This chapter examines the heroic efforts to make cryptocurrency environmentally sustainable and socially responsible, drawing uncomfortable parallels to attempts to domesticate mythical beasts. Features the tale of a well-meaning unicorn ranch that promised "free-range, organic, carbon-neutral magic" and collapsed when someone checked the math. Also covers the recurring phenomenon of crypto founders who promise to "bank the unbanked" while unbanking the already-banked.
