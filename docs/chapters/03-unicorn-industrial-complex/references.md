# References: The Unicorn-Industrial Complex

1. [Unicorn (finance)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Unicorn_(finance)) - Wikipedia - History and analysis of the billion-dollar startup designation, including growth trends and notable examples central to this chapter's economic allegory.

2. [Venture capital](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Venture_capital) - Wikipedia - Comprehensive overview of VC funding structures, valuation methods, and the speculative dynamics that create unicorn valuations.

3. [Dot-com bubble](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dot-com_bubble) - Wikipedia - Detailed account of the late-1990s technology speculation bubble, providing historical precedent for the magical thinking this chapter satirizes.

4. The Power Law: Venture Capital and the Making of the New Future - Sebastian Mallaby - Penguin Press - Authoritative history of venture capital's evolution, documenting the incentive structures that reward unicorn-hunting over sustainable business building.

5. Irrational Exuberance (3rd Edition) - Robert J. Shiller - Princeton University Press - Nobel laureate's analysis of speculative bubbles and herd behavior in financial markets, directly applicable to unicorn valuation dynamics.

6. [Global Unicorn Club](https://www.cbinsights.com/research-unicorn-companies) - CB Insights - Live database tracking over 1,200 unicorn companies worldwide with valuations, investors, and industry breakdowns.

7. [Venture Capital Funnel](https://www.kauffman.org/resources/entrepreneurship-policy-digest/) - Kauffman Foundation - Research on startup survival rates and the statistical reality behind unicorn aspirations, providing the data this chapter's satire rests upon.

8. [WeWork: How a $47B Unicorn Lost Its Magic](https://www.investopedia.com/the-downfall-of-wework-ipo-4771084) - Investopedia - Case study of a unicorn's spectacular deflation, exemplifying the gap between valuation and value that defines the chapter's central argument.

9. [The Age of the Unicorn](https://hbr.org/2020/12/the-age-of-the-unicorn) - Harvard Business Review - Analysis of what drives unicorn creation and whether the designation indicates genuine innovation or financial engineering.

10. [Theranos and the Unicorn Problem](https://www.sec.gov/news/press-release/2018-41) - U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission - Official enforcement action documenting how unicorn mythology enabled a $9 billion fraud, the chapter's most uncomfortable real-world parallel.
