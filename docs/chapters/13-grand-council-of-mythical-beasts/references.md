# References: The Grand Council of Mythical Beasts

1. [United Nations General Assembly](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Nations_General_Assembly) - Wikipedia - Structure and proceedings of the world's primary intergovernmental deliberative body, the institutional template for the chapter's mythical beast summit.

2. [AI safety](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AI_safety) - Wikipedia - Overview of research addressing risks from advanced AI systems, the existential concern that drives the mythical beasts to convene their unprecedented council.

3. [Paris Agreement](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paris_Agreement) - Wikipedia - The landmark 2015 climate accord, a model for international cooperation on existential threats that this chapter's beasts attempt to replicate with comparable success.

4. Governance of AI: A Manifesto - Wendell Wallach - MIT Press - Framework for governing transformative AI technologies, providing the policy backdrop for the council's deliberations on AI-driven obsolescence.

5. The Uninhabitable Earth - David Wallace-Wells - Tim Duggan Books - Unflinching account of collective failure to address climate change, paralleling the chapter's portrayal of mythical beasts debating existential threats without taking action.

6. [AI Governance Frameworks](https://www.weforum.org/projects/ai-governance-alliance/) - World Economic Forum - International governance initiatives for AI policy, the real-world equivalent of the chapter's fictional Grand Council.

7. [EU AI Act](https://artificialintelligenceact.eu/) - EU AI Act Hub - Comprehensive guide to the European Union's AI regulation, the most ambitious real attempt at the kind of governance the chapter's mythical beasts discuss.

8. [AI Ethics Guidelines Global Inventory](https://algorithmwatch.org/en/ai-ethics-guidelines-global-inventory/) - AlgorithmWatch - Database tracking AI ethics guidelines worldwide, documenting humanity's own version of the council's inability to agree on anything binding.

9. [The Existential Risk of AI](https://www.safe.ai/work/statement-on-ai-risk) - Center for AI Safety - The 2023 one-sentence statement on AI extinction risk signed by hundreds of researchers, the real-world urgency behind the chapter's satirical summit.

10. [Conference of the Parties (COP)](https://unfccc.int/process/bodies/supreme-bodies/conference-of-the-parties-cop) - United Nations Climate Change - Documentation of annual climate summits, providing a template for grand international gatherings that produce impressive communiqués and modest action.
